Kim Zolciak Shares &quot;Unedited&quot; Topless Photo Via Instagram

 Posted on March 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  205 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Perhaps it's all about good lighting? Kim Zolciak offered fans a behind-the-scenes look at her latest photo shoot with an un-retouched (and topless!) photo, shared via Instagram on Tuesday, Sept. 1.

"Unedited photo from last weeks photo shoot with my favorite @mrdblanks [photographer Derek Blanks]," wrote Zolciak, 37, who modeled Diesel jeans and a strategically placed cowboy hat that barely covered her bodacious body. "I'm a country girl at heart."

Pointing out her slim physique, Don't Be Tardy's leading lady also shouted-out 310 Nutrition for "making it easy to stay in shape," adding the hashtag #EveryMorning310Shake.

While the mom of six did indeed look trim in the photo, the use of the word "unedited" garnered mixed results from commenters. While one wrote, "Damn Kim! That's a hot photo!" and another said, "YOU GO GIRL! You look fabulous!" one more countered, "Unedited my ass."

Nevertheless, as one fan pointed out, Zolciak, who married the Atlanta Falcons' Kroy Biermann in 2011, can be applauded for keeping it real with her fan base: "She has admitted to a boob job and a tummy tuck."
