Kamaru Usman aka Kamarudeen “Kamaru” Usman is a Nigerian-American MMA fighter. As of 2022, Kamaru Usman’s net worth is $3 million. He turned professional MMA (mixed martial artist) fighter after pursuing his career in wrestling.

He used to wrestle in the freestyle segment and also is a graduate with a folkstyle wrestling degree. He is currently employed as a fighter in the UFC (ultimate fighting championship).

Usman has earned his reputation as one of the best of all-time welterweight fighters. He is also sitting at the number 2 spot in the pound-for-pound rankings enlisted by UFC (last updated on March 23, 2021).

Kamaru Usman Wiki/Biography

Born on 11 May 1987, Kamaru Usman’s age is 35 Years Old as of 2022. He was born in Auchi, Nigeria. Kamaru holds American and Nigerian by nationality and Muslim by religion. Kamaru has a black belt in Brazilian Jiu-jitsu.
