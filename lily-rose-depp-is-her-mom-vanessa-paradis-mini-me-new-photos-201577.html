Lily-Rose Depp Is Her Mom Vanessa Paradis' Mini-Me: New Photos

 Posted on May 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  226 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

There’s mother-daughter duos that look alike — and then there’s Vanessa Paradis and her daughter Lily-Rose Depp. The stunning duo lent their genetically gifted selves for Karl Lagerfeld’s Chanel Haute Couture show in Paris on Tuesday, July 7, and confirmed that they’re practically twins.

Johnny Depp’s daughter, who recently celebrated her Sweet 16, donned a chic black strapless gown from the fashion house, while her mom, 42, opted for a gold suit. The pair both sported their signature blonde bobs. 

Lily-Rose couldn’t be more grown up, either. The sophisticated teen participated in the show, playing casino games at the tables which flanked the runway. “all in, sir! Thank you @chanelofficial !!!!” she captioned an Instagram of herself playing alongside male model Baptiste Giabiconi.

Lily Collins also posted a snap with the gorgeous 16-year-old. “Les deux Lily’s. Honored to link arms and grace the runway with this Lil @lilyrose_depp #EnglishFrenchAmericanroses…,” she captioned their selfie. 

Paradis’ daughter made her modeling debut this past April, posing for Australia-based magazine Oyster. “The daughter of Johnny Depp and Vanessa Paradis, Lily-Rose Depp, has the face of an angel and a magnetic presence beyond her young years,” the magazine wrote.

Paradis was in a long-term partnership with Depp for 14 years until they split in June 2012. They’re also parents to 13-year-old son Jack. Depp married wife Amber Heard in February 2015.
