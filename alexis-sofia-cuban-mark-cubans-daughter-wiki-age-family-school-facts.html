Alexis Sofia Cuban Wiki; Age, Birthday, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

Mark and Tiffany first met at the gym and married after five years of dating in 2002. Their private wedding ceremony was held in Barbados. Her father, Mark, is the owner of the NBA side Dallas Mavericks, co-owner of 2929 Entertainment, and the chairman of AXS TV.

Meanwhile, her mother, Tiffany, 51, is a media personality who worked as an advertising executive. Alexis grew up with two younger siblings; a sister named Alyssa Cuban and a brother named Jake Cuban.

She is Ashkenazi Jewish by ethnicity, and her great-grandparents emigrated from Russia to the United States. Alexis’s birth sign is Libra.
