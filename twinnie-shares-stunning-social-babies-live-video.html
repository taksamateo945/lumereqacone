Twinnie Shares Stunning 'Social Babies' Live Video

 Posted on February 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  224 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Last month rising British Country Music talent Twinnie shared the breathtaking live and acoustic version of her massive hit single ‘Social Babies’.

The stripped back version allowed for the Yorkshire singer/songwriter’s powerful vocals to really shine while allowing the poignant lyrics about the world of social media and the pit falls within the use of the platform to really come through.

And now, Twinnie has shared the stunning live video for the gorgeous acoustic rendition. The captivating clip was filmed and recorded at Hoxta HQ, in London.

Speaking about the video and track, the triple-threat performer says: “I wanted to reimagine the track. Although the song is up-tempo I originally wrote it slow then just speeded up, it’s how a lot of my songs are written. I think it allows you to hear the emotion and the message of the song a lot more. The track has a strong message inspired by the online culture that we all live in, I wanted to shine a light on social media and all the things it is and not”.
